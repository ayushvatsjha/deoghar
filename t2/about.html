Our Chess Journey

Founded in 2016

Deoghar Chess Academy, located in the vibrant cities of New Delhi and Deoghar, extends its services of online and offline chess classes to aspiring players in the region. With instructors passionate about cultivating strategic thinking and decision-making skills, the academy is dedicated to empowering its students to excel in the game of chess. Deoghar Chess Academy strives to create an engaging and enriching environment for all levels of players, nurturing their passion and potential as they embark on their journey in the world of chess.
